Draft Analysis Dashboard

This dashboard displays the success rate of draft picks by different teams since 1989.

Methodology

The success of draft picks is determined based on a weighted score derived from z-scores of VORP, WS, and BPM. The weighted score threshold was set to 0.3 to classify successful picks.
